Monika Duszyńska

Monika Duszyńska is a lawyer specializing in legal advice for the pharmaceutical sector, medical devices and health protection.Since 1999, he has been supporting pharmaceutical companies, producers of medical devices and health -promoting foods, their subsidiaries and distributors in matters requiring specialized reimbursement knowledge, pharmaceutical law and other sectoral regulations. Her practice includes both strategic consulting and comprehensive regulatory service.

He advises on matters related to the registration of medicinal products, trading in products and medical devices, their advertising, production (also contract), import, distribution and compliance - also in relations with representatives of medical professions and patient organizations.He specializes in reimbursement and consulting in the field of intellectual property, including R&D contracts, licenses for registration documentation and copyright. She participated in many M&A transactions, conducting legal audits (due diligence), including regulations regarding, among others medicines, supplements and medical devices.

He actively publishes and comments on issues regarding clinical trials, reimbursement of drugs, biological substitutes and legislation regarding non -standard medicine.Awarded for years byChambers & Partners (Life Sciences 2025 - Europe Band 2). A graduate of the Faculty of Law at the University of Warsaw and Italian philology. He is fluent in English and also provides legal services in French, Italian and Spanish.

Cooperation

From 2015, we have been cooperating withThe "Pharmacy Polska Pharmacy" Chamber, a leading industry association associating several dozen pharmaceutical companies and pharmaceutical wholesalers.
