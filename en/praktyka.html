What We Do?

    We support companies in the pharmaceutical and medical device sectors.

    We provide comprehensive legal services at every stage of a product’s lifecycle:

  • R&D and clinical trials
  • Registration, manufacturing, marketing and advertising
  • Market entry and reimbursement
  • Distribution, sales and compliance

    • Our expertise, built on many years of experience, covers:

  • Sector-specific regulations
  • Compliance
  • Contract law and intellectual property

How We Work?

Our distinctive feature is in -depth knowledge not only of sector regulations and economic law, but also market practice. We know and understand the problems and challenges of the medical and medical devices industry, as well as how they evolved with legislative changes and case law.

We attach particular importance to the highest quality services. This means:

  • thorough analysis of each issue, including risk and optimal solutions,
  • wide and material justification of our recommendations,
  • completeness, consistency and precision of prepared documents,
  • Continuous improvement of methods, including the use of artificial intelligence.
