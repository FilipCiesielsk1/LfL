Participation in the meeting of the Polish Coalition of Oncological Patients

On June 10, 2016, Monika Duszyńska took part in a meeting of patients with journalists organized by the Polish coalition of oncological patients. The meeting was devoted to, among others patients' activity in parliamentary teams, and system and organizational changes in oncology. Monika Duszyńska said, among others On regulation regarding rescue access to medical technologies, which replaced the so -called custom chemotherapy.
