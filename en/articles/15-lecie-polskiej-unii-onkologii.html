Debate of the Polish Oncology Union on the occasion of the 15th anniversary

On January 28, 2016, Monika Duszyńska took part in the System-Expert debate on the occasion of the 15th anniversary of the Polish Oncology Union, organized by the Polish Oncology Union together with the Green Communications agency. Krzysztof Łanda, deputy minister of health, dr Janusz Meder, president of PUO, and Marek Belka, president of the NBP also took part in the debate. Monika spoke, among others on the desired standards of law in the area of health protection.
