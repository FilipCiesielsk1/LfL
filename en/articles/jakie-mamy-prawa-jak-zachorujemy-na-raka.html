What rights do we have when we get cancer?

On June 14, an information and educational project was started with a straight about breast cancer consisting of a series of 10 episodes and 10 conversations with experts. In the first episode of the series, Monika Duszyńska raises such topics as: rights and obligations in access to medical services for oncological patients and legal aspects related to access to the drug program. He also answers questions about the Dilo Card, the basic laws of the patient, documents needed in the hospital and dealing with organizational and administrative matters at work during illness.
