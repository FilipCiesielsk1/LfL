Workshops for patient organizations - HTA in Poland and the European Union

On January 27, 2020, Monika Duszyńska co -hosted workshops on the subject of medical technology assessment. 26 representatives from 16 oncological patients from all over Poland took part in the meeting organized by the Polish coalition of oncological patients. The purpose of the workshop was to familiarize the organization's leaders with the practical side of HTA functioning in Poland and other European countries.
